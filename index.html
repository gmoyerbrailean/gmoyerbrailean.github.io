Gregory Moyerbrailean

I am a graduate student at Wayne State University in Detroit, Michigan. My work involves developing computational models to analyze functional genomics data, and using them to understand the impact genetic variants have on gene regulation and expression.


In my free time I love spending time with my wife, who is a medical resident at Scripps Mercy Hospital in San Diego, California. Together, we enjoy walking along the beach, exploring San Diego, and traveling to new places.
